I went to Lisbon for the Perl Toolchain Summit in 2024 and I made sure to tack
on time to be a tourist.  I guess 2023 or 2024 is when I started to realize I
had to *just make sure* I spent time seeing the places I was going.  Twenty
years late, but better than never.  Lisbon was terrific.  Beautiful, good
weather, great food, and pretty affordable!  I almost didn't go to Cintra, but
I did, and I'm really grateful for it!

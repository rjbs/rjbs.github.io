I went to St. Louis for The Strange Loop, a now-defunct conference about topics
in computing.  It was a very cool conference, covering lots of things you
wouldn't find many other places.  While there, I ate BBQ and went to the
amazing City Museum.  Mostly, though, it felt *so empty*!

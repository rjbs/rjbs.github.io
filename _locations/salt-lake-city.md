I wish I had spent more time exploring SLC, but I was there for a conference,
and it felt like everything was far away and uphill and the air was thin.
Crossing the street, you'd pick up a flag from the lamp post and wave it while
you crossed so that cars could see you while they zoomed down the huge roads.
I should go back.

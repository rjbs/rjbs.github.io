In 2013, we went to Charleston, to The Citadel, for my brother-in-law's
graduation from the Naval Academy.  It was a nice trip, but I gotta say by far
the biggest hit was Dell'z, where we went for a vegetarian meal amid a lot of
family activities that weren't super vegetarian-friendly.  The food was great,
and the person running the place was great, and was really sweet to Marty.

For decades, Vienna was on my "to visit" list, but I kept not getting there.
Finally, in 2024, I was going to be in Brussels for work (for FOSDEM), and I
tacked on a week in Vienna.  It was a *great* trip.  I saw lots of museums, I
ate cake every day, and I caught up with some friends.

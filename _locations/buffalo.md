Buffalo is probably the best example, in my memory, of how much more I can
enjoy a place by going there *to* enjoy it.  Obvious, but easy for me to
forget.  I went there for a YAPC in 2004 or so, and went into the city once or
twice, and went to Anchor Bar with the conference.  Meh.  Then in 2023, I went
there just for fun, with a friend, with some visiting with another friend
there.  It was great!  I'd go again, even.

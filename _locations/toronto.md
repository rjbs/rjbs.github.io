I went to Toronto in 2005 for YAPC.  It was one of my first YAPCs, and I had a
great time.  At many conferences, I don't see enough of the city -- and I
didn't see enough of Toronto -- but I did a much better time enjoying the city
than I often do, and I left with lots of good memories.

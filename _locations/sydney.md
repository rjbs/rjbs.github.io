I've gone to Melbourne often for work.  On a number of those trips, I stopped
in Sydney on the way in, or on the weekend during the trip.  Once you've
traveled halfway around the world, you might as well make the most of it!
Sydney is terrific!  Beautiful city, lots to see, very easy to get around.  I
haven't gotten tired of it yet.

I was in Orlando first in 1989, visiting family but also going to Disney.  I
was there the weekend they opened Disney MGM Studios, which was Disney's answer
to Universal Studios.  It seemed absolutely swamped, but then nobody was going
because of the rain, so we went and got in.  Since, I've been back for
conferences.  Cuban food was on point.

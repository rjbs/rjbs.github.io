Las Vegas!  I have never had much interest in it, but I went when my dad was
inducted into the Rugby Hall of Fame and there was a ceremony there.  What a
weird place.  I feel like maybe I could go again and try to just take in all
the weirdness, but I think I'd be too cynical (because Vegas is too cynical).
Highlights: Australia v. Fiji game, In-N-Out Burger, seeing Jay Shirley, and
getting really good brunch buffet at a place run by a guy from McAdoo, PA.

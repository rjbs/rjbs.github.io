Was this my only time in Maine?  Maybe.  I went for my college roommate's
wedding.  It was hot and traffic was bad, but Gloria and I listened to an
enormous amount of the radio play of Hitchhiker's Guide, and then it was a
friend's wedding, so what's not to love?  As for Monmouth itself… I remember
only the room the wedding was in.
